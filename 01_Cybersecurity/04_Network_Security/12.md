**Internet cables** are physical cables used to **transmit data between devices and networks**, enabling internet access.

### 🔌 Main types of internet cables

#### 1. Ethernet cables

- Most common for home and office networks
- Connect devices to routers or switches
- Types include: Cat5e, Cat6, Cat7
- Reliable and fast (up to multi-gigabit speeds)

#### 2. Coaxial cables

- Used by cable internet providers
- Also used for TV signals
- Moderate speed and good shielding

#### 3. Fiber optic cables

- Use light to transmit data
- Extremely fast and long-distance capable
- Immune to electrical interference
- Used in modern high-speed internet connections

#### 4. Telephone (DSL) cables

- Use traditional phone lines
- Slower compared to fiber and Ethernet
- Still used in some areas

### ⚙️ How they differ

- **Speed**: Fiber > Ethernet > Coaxial > DSL
- **Distance**: Fiber works best over long distances
- **Cost**: Fiber is usually more expensive
