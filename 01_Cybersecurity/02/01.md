Business Continuity Planning (BCP) in cybersecurity focuses on ensuring organizations can maintain critical operations during
and after cyber incidents like ransomware attacks or data breaches. It integrates risk management, incident response, and
recovery strategies to minimize downtime and financial loss.
[cyberdefensemagazine](https://www.cyberdefensemagazine.com/how-to-develop-a-business-continuity-plan-for-cyber-security-a-step-by-step-guide/)

## Core Purpose

BCP prepares businesses for disruptions by identifying essential functions and protecting them against cyber threats such as
DDoS attacks, malware, or insider risks. Effective plans reduce recovery time by up to 35% and cut costs through proactive
measures like regular backups.
[bryghtpath](https://bryghtpath.com/integrating-cybersecurity-into-business-continuity-planning-a-comprehensive-guide/)

## Key Components

- **Risk Assessment**: Conduct a Business Impact Analysis (BIA) to pinpoint critical assets, like customer databases or IT
  infrastructure, and evaluate threats' likelihood and impact.
  [upguard](https://www.upguard.com/blog/business-continuity-plan)
- **Incident Response**: Define steps for detecting, containing, and remediating breaches, including communication protocols
  for stakeholders. [pecb](https://pecb.com/en/article/link-between-business-continuity-and-cybersecurity)
- **Recovery Strategies**: Implement data backups, redundant systems, and workflows to restore operations quickly.
  [kratikal](https://kratikal.com/blog/the-role-of-cybersecurity-in-effective-business-continuity-planning/)
- **Testing and Training**: Run tabletop exercises, functional drills, and full-scale simulations at least twice yearly to
  validate the plan.
  [cyberdefensemagazine](https://www.cyberdefensemagazine.com/how-to-develop-a-business-continuity-plan-for-cyber-security-a-step-by-step-guide/)

## Implementation Steps

Start with a comprehensive threat assessment, then develop policies integrating cybersecurity into overall BCP. Regularly
update plans annually or after major changes to address evolving risks like new ransomware variants.
[bryghtpath](https://bryghtpath.com/integrating-cybersecurity-into-business-continuity-planning-a-comprehensive-guide/)

## Benefits

Organizations with robust BCP recover faster, ensure compliance, and protect sensitive data, making cybersecurity a backbone
of resilience. [securityscorecard](https://securityscorecard.com/zh-TW/blog/business-continuity-plan/)
