Physical destruction is the most reliable way to ensure data on HDDs and SSDs cannot be recovered, as it physically damages
storage media beyond forensic repair. This is especially crucial for SSDs, where overwriting is unreliable due to
wear-leveling and TRIM.

## HDD Destruction Methods

HDDs store data magnetically on spinning platters, so target the platters and controller board.

- **Drilling**: Use a power drill with metal/masonry bits to bore multiple holes (at least 5-7) through the platters and PCB.
  Aim for the center and edges. [reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/storage/comments/13sfejb/cheapeasy_way_to_destroy_drives/)
- **Hammering/Smashing**: Strike platters repeatedly with a hammer or sledgehammer on a hard surface until they shatter into
  fragments. Wear safety gear to avoid flying shards.
  [shredall.co](https://www.shredall.co.uk/blogs/blog/how-to-destroy-old-hard-drives)
- **Shredding**: Feed into an industrial shredder that grinds into particles <2mm. DIY alternatives include angle grinders or
  disassembly followed by shredding platters. [crownrms](https://www.crownrms.com/uk/insights/hard-drive-shredding/)
- **Degaussing + Physical**: First degauss (strong magnet to scramble magnetic data), then physically destroy. Degaussing
  alone renders HDDs inoperable but pair with smashing for certainty.
  [crownrms](https://www.crownrms.com/uk/insights/hard-drive-shredding/)
- **Incineration**: Burn in a high-heat furnace (>1000°C) to melt components, but requires professional facilities.

## SSD Destruction Methods

SSDs use NAND flash chips, so focus on pulverizing all chips.

- **Crushing**: Use a hydraulic crusher or heavy industrial press to smash into powder, targeting every chip.
  [xrecycling.co](https://xrecycling.co.uk/ssd-destruction-secure-certified-methods/)
- **Shredding**: Industrial shredders reduce to tiny fragments (<2mm). More effective than crushing for high-security needs.
  [crownrms](https://www.crownrms.com/uk/insights/hard-drive-shredding/)
- **Drilling/Grinding**: Drill through each NAND chip with metal bits, or use a Dremel/cold chisel to split/grind chips.
  Repeat on both PCB sides.
  [ifixit](https://www.ifixit.com/Guide/How+to+safely+and+securely+destroy+data+on+SSDs+and+memory+sticks/142798)
- **Incineration**: Expose to extreme heat to destroy circuits and melt chips.
  [xrecycling.co](https://xrecycling.co.uk/ssd-destruction-secure-certified-methods/)
- **Disassembly + Chip Destruction**: Open casing, chisel or grind individual memory chips.

## Precautions

Always wear PPE (goggles, gloves, mask). Work in ventilated areas to avoid dust/chemicals. For compliance (e.g., NIST, DoD),
use certified services with chain-of-custody. Before destruction, remove/reuse reusable parts like RAM if present. Physical
methods preclude data recovery but generate e-waste—recycle remnants.
[xrecycling.co](https://xrecycling.co.uk/ssd-destruction-secure-certified-methods/)

In summary, shredding or crushing by professionals offers top security for both drive types, while DIY drilling/hammering
suffices for personal use with thorough execution.
